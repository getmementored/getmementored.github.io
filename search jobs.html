PROCESS TO CONNECT WITH MENTORS

Objective: To provide students with tailored guidance by connecting them with industry experts. This process ensures efficient matching, student validation, secure payment, and a satisfaction guarantee for a valuable experience.

Step 1: Form Submission
Student Application: Students fill out an online form providing their details, educational background, career aspirations, and specific questions they seek answers to.
LINK TO THE FORM

Step 2: Understanding Student's Requirements and Expert Matching
Review and Analysis:
Evaluate student applications to understand their requirements and expectations thoroughly. Identify the relevant industry expert(s) based on the student's needs and preferences.

Step 3: Expert Details Sharing
Expert Introduction: Share detailed information about the selected industry expert(s) with the student, including their professional background and expertise. Provide a brief overview of what the student can expect from the interaction.

Step 4: Student Validation and Payment
Student Confirmation: Confirm the expert choice with the student and ensure they agree to proceed with the selected expert. Obtain validation from the student to initiate the payment process.
Payment Collection: Collect a nominal fee from the student using a secure payment gateway before scheduling the call. Provide a confirmation of payment receipt to the student.

Step 5: Scheduling the Call
Mutual Availability: Coordinate with both the student and the expert to find a suitable time for the consultation call. Send calendar invitations to both parties, including the date, time, and meeting platform details.

Step 6: Interaction and Satisfaction Guarantee
Consultation Call: Facilitate the scheduled call between the student and the expert, ensuring a smooth and productive conversation. Provide a secure and confidential environment for the discussion.
Post-Interaction Feedback: Gather feedback from both the student and the expert after the interaction to assess satisfaction levels and gather insights for improvement. Satisfaction Guarantee: If the student is not satisfied with the consultation, initiate the refund process promptly. Ensure that the refund is processed efficiently and the student is informed about the completion of the refund.

Step 7: Continuous Improvement and Follow-up
Feedback Analysis:
Regularly analyze feedback received from students and experts to identify patterns and areas of improvement. Use feedback to enhance the matching process and overall user experience.
Follow-up Support: Provide additional resources or support to students who require further assistance based on their interactions. Maintain communication with both students and experts to build a reliable network for future interactions.

By following this structured process, students can receive personalized guidance from industry experts while ensuring their satisfaction and the security of their financial transactions. Continuous improvement and a commitment to customer satisfaction are key elements of this process.
